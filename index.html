Geophysical Engineer Graduate
Institut Teknologi Sepuluh November

I am a graduate of Geophysical Engineering Major from Institut Teknologi Sepuluh November, Surabaya. My field of study is towards petrophysical analysis, and also near surface field survey. My work included evaluating exploration wells in Borneo and geothermal wells in West Java, also evaluating landslide potential in several powerplants at West Java. My previous focuses on application of newly geophysical method on real life case.
